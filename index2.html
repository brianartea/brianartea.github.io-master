How I Make Websites

Planning, designing and development.

Planning

Planning is the most important part of making a website. A well planned website makes making the website much easier. There's nothing wrong with using a great template.

Designing

This is the hardest part because I sit with the client or email back and forth different ideas and make five to seven website mock-ups until they see one they like. Sometimes it takes more sometimes it takes less.

Development

Once the design is out of the way and know how we want the website to function, it's time to make the website come to life and look goood in all browsers (Google Chrome, Mozilla Firefox, Microst Edge, Apple Safari) and need to look great on all smartphones, tablets, laptop and desktop computers.

Production

This is where the website gets the final tune-up.
